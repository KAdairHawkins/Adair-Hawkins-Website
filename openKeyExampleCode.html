TJ Person

Chief Executive Officer

As CEO and founder, TJ Person has made OpenKey the leader in universal mobile key access technology. With more than 17 years of experience in technology, business management and marketing, TJ knows what it takes to make a company successful. He is known for both his strategic leadership and marketing innovator capabilities.

Under his leadership, OpenKey has partnered with the largest global BLE lock companies and successfully raised millions in strategic investments from some of the largest hotel management and ownership companies such as Ashford Hospitality Trust (NYSE MKT: AHT).

Prior to OpenKey, TJ was the founder and CEO of Koupon Media, the industry’s first one-time-use mobile coupon platform built for brands and agencies. In 2005, TJ co-founded Mango Mobile, a leading mobile marketing agency where he was responsible for global marketing strategy. Omnicom Group acquired the company in 2007 after record growth.

TJ currently serves as Chairman of Koupon Media and as an advisor to FanPrint, an e-commerce platform built exclusively for passionate sports fans. He holds a U.S. patent (US 9,076,083) for Secure One-Time Printing of Coupons.

Ryan Douglas

VP of Operations

Ryan Douglas joined OpenKey in 2015 bringing leadership skills, management experience and a vision to take this rapidly growing company to the next level. In his role as Vice President of Operations, Ryan oversees product support, infrastructure needs and operating efficiency for the company.

With more than 15 years of experience, Ryan has held executive roles with companies including Expedia, Barton Creek Resort & Spa, IQPLUS Foods and Austin Rowing Club. He is also the co-founder of CampRewards.com, a new way for people to search and book camping destinations.

Ryan volunteers regularly for a number of causes and organizations including: Phoenix Society for Burned Survivors, Circle of Friends at Dell Children’s Hospital, Parkland Hospital Burn Unit, American Camp Association, Texas Scottish Rite Hospital for Children, Camp John Marc and Children’s Hospital Burn Camp.

He earned his MBA in Business Administration from Baylor University and his B.S. degree from the University of North Texas.

Brian Shedd

VP Sales & Marketing

Leading OpenKey’s Sales and Marketing teams, Brian Shedd brings senior level expertise from both the Las Vegas Strip and Madison Avenue. As an executive with Mandalay Resort Group, Brian was responsible for marketing over 7000 rooms, 2 casinos, 9 restaurants and 3 entertainment venues.

Since joining OpenKey’s executive team, Brian’s action oriented and hands on approach, has played a key role in creating competitive differentiation for the company and overseeing rapid growth and expansion into every major stay brand and independent hotels nationwide.

Before joining OpenKey, Brian spend nearly a decade leading business development and integration efforts for various Omnicom agencies including the Radiate Group. He has also held senior level posts in independent agencies as Managing Director of Calise Partners and Chief Marketing Officer of FaceTime Strategy.

Brian holds a Master’s degree in Hospitality Administration from the University of Nevada in Las Vegas and a B.S degree in Business Administration from San Jose State University.

Jacob Liggett

Director of Sales

As the Director of Sales at OpenKey, Jacob Liggett is responsible for managing all aspects of lead generation, business development and strategic partnerships. Jacob and his team constitute the ‘front line’ of OpenKey growth and development, working closely with Marketing, Operations and the Product teams to grow OpenKey market share.

Jacob’s love of technology and his passion for developing long-term business relationships has helped fuel OpenKey’s growth. Not only has this increased the customer base, but has helped find new ways to make existing customers more profitable.

Jon Alsup

Vice President of Product

Jon joined OpenKey in March of 2015 as the Senior Director of Technology. He is responsible for overseeing the technology development behind the universal mobile key application including all lock SDK integrations and feature and functional evolutions.

Jon’s passion for technology fuels him to continually improve the consumer and client experience with the OpenKey app through more insightful tools and user interfaces. Under his leadership, four versions of the universal mobile access key and white label platforms have been developed and launched on iTunes and Google Play.

Jon already has a long history of technology expertise and launching startups. At the age of 19, he started his own crowdfunding platform called Acusta Launch. He also served as the Business and Operations Manager for Docudesk, a multi-million dollar company, where he was responsible for web development and software project management.

Jon holds a B.S. degree in Consumer Economics from West Texas A&M University.

Chris Hickingbottom

Vice President of Engineering

Chris joined OpenKey in January 2017 bringing more than 10 years of programming and management experience as a technological visionary. As the VP of Engineering, Chris is responsible for developing security protocols, mobile and web applications, third-party integrations and more.

With advanced knowledge of the entire development process, he keeps our clients’ and their guests’ security top of mind. Chris enjoys inspiring people with his passion for developing new and innovative technologies.

Before joining OpenKey, Chris co-found Pharmedio, a company that connects specialty and compounding pharmacies with their sales forces in real time. As the CTO, he led the day-to-day operations of the development team and acted as the technological voice of the company.
